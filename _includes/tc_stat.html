Historical Tropical Cyclone Statistics

TC Statistics

  • Statistics are calculated from best tracks archived in the International Best Track Archive for Climate Stewardship (IBTrACS, available online HERE).
  • In the Southern Hemishphere, tropical cyclone seasons are defined to start on July 1 of the previous year.
    For example, the 1980 season ran from July 1, 1979 through June 30, 1980.
